At LaitCo, we have a passion for quality milk and supporting local farmers. Since our establishment in 2005, we have been committed to providing efficient and fair milk collection services, ensuring that every producer is rewarded fairly for their hard work.

Our journey started humbly with a small group of determined farmers aiming to create a company that values and respects the efforts of dairy farmers. Over the years, we have grown to become a key player in the dairy industry, establishing strong partnerships with passionate farmers and meeting the needs of our customers.

Values and Commitments

At LaitCo, we are guided by strong values that shape our business on a daily basis. Integrity is at the core of everything we do, maintaining honest and transparent relationships with our farmers and customers. We also believe in sustainability, embracing environmentally-friendly practices throughout our supply chain.

Our commitment to quality is unwavering. We work closely with our farmers to ensure fresh and nutritious milk, adhering to the highest standards of food safety. Furthermore, we take pride in supporting our local farmers, contributing to the vitality of rural communities.

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to provide efficient and fair milk collection services while creating added value for our farmers and customers. We strive to be a trusted partner, offering tailor-made solutions to meet the specific needs of each farmer. Our vision is to play a key role in the sustainable development of the dairy industry, fostering the economic growth of farmers and ensuring a reliable supply of quality milk.

We take pride in our heritage and commitment to excellence. By choosing LaitCo, you opt for reliable service, complete transparency, and a long-term trusted relationship.
